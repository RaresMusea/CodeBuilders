CodeBuilders is a platform that will help you build your career in software development

Programming is a pleasant activity, but very often it becomes stressful when we don't know what to learn, or where we should learn from.

The Internet would be the answer to both of the 2 questions above, but many times we don't even realize that we are learning from some non-compliant, depreciated, non-updated materials according to current standards.

How would it be if there was a platform where people could learn a topic from this vast field in a relatively easy way, benefiting from periodically updated resources and technical support for any of their uncertainties?

CodeBuilders has the solution to all of these questions.

Learning made easy

CodeBuilders is a learning platform dedicated to all those who are passionate about programming, regardless of whether we are talking about beginners or experts.

Why would an expert want to learn programming again?

Well, in a world where technologies, platforms, and development environments evolve at such a fast pace, it is essential, even for an expert, to document himself, refresh his knowledge, or even learn from zero a new technology appeared. The desire to surpass oneself makes the difference between a programmer motivated by passion and one motivated only by his salary.

We appreciate, promote and respect effort and perseverance. We are a team of qualified professionals who will help you throughout your learning process. The information should be accessible to anyone who wants to use it, which is why we believe that sharing knowledge does not put you at a disadvantage, on the contrary, it helps you self-evaluate.

By the end of our courses, the candidates will come into possession of an average amount of knowledge, which will certainly help them find a job related to the course they followed. The difference between this platform and any other is that it is free. We built this community with dedication, passion and devotion. We will do everything in our power to help you in this long and lengthy process of personal development, or in your career!
